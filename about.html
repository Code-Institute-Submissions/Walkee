Why Get Walkee?

Walkee is the app where you can instantly find other pups nearby who need company on their walks. Dogs are social animials and need to spend time with other pooches to improve both mental and physical health! You can chat, arrange where you'll meet and even track your doggies exercise each week! Not only will you get to make new friends but your dog will also get much more physical exercise through play. Don't let your dog be lonely, get Walkee today!

The Benifits of walking with other Dogs

  • Impoved exercise with lots of play
  • Walking with other dogs will improve their socialisation
  • Having a furbuddy to walk with will alleviate sadness.
  • Lateral training from older dogs
